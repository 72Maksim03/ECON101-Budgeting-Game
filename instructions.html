How to Play the Budgeting Game

In this budgeting simulation, you will manage a fictional person's finances over 12 months, divided into 4 weeks each. Your goal is to maintain a balance between spending, saving, and investing while keeping your health and entertainment levels up.

Basic Gameplay:

  • Monthly Salary: You receive a fixed salary at the start of each month.
  • Housing: Choose a housing option each month and pay the rent. Missing rent means losing the game.
  • Transportation: Select how you commute — public transport or buy/use a car. Cars must be purchased first.
  • Entertainment: Use money to maintain your entertainment level, which affects your health.
  • Health: Manage your health by balancing spending and using hospital services if needed.
  • Investments: You can invest money to earn returns monthly.
  • Debt: You may take loans but be careful — interest accrues every two weeks, and debt over $2000 ends the game.
  • Weekly Events: Random events may affect your money, health, or entertainment.

Important Notes:

  • You must pay your rent every month or the game ends immediately.
  • Entertainment drops each week depending on your housing and transportation choices.
  • If your entertainment drops too low, your health will also deteriorate.
  • Health below a critical level may force hospital visits, reducing your salary temporarily.
  • Gym and streaming subscriptions can help maintain your entertainment and health levels but come at a monthly cost.
  • Balance your spending wisely to avoid debt and stay healthy.
